Frequently Asked Questions

Is the system scalable for future expansion?
A good HMS should be designed to accommodate the growth of the hospital, adding new functionalities and supporting increased data volume.
What security measures are in place to protect patient data?
The system should have robust security measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits, to protect patient information.

Can the system handle insurance claims?
Yes, many HMSs have features to process insurance claims, including verification and submission.
How does the HMS handle staff management?
It manages staff information, duty schedules, and tracks attendance. It may also have modules for payroll and performance evaluation.

Patients can usually access their medical records through a secure patient portal, which may include test results, prescription history, and treatment plans.
